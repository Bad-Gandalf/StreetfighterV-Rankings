Street Fighter V Top 50 Professionals Dashboard
Correlation between Lifetime Rank and Actual Score

Lifetime rank is based on a players total points accrued over all previous seasons of tournament play i.e. the player with the most lifetime points will be ranked at number 1. The actual score represents the points accrued for the current season. There is a strong correlation between lifetime rank and actual score. There are a few outliers such as Nemo.

Lifetime Score by Country

This is the share of players lifetime points shared by their countries of origin. Japan is clearly dominates the sport followed in second by the United States.

Total Lifetime Score By Gender

This chart displays total lifetime points accrued when using either a male or female character. It shows male characters have obtained 32% more lifetime points than female characters.

Average Lifetime Score By Gender

The chart displays the average lifetime score per character gender. It divides the total lifetime points accrued by the frequency of usage of each gender. This shows that on average a female character will accrue more lifetime points than male characters.

Total Lifetime Score by Character

This bar chart displays the total lifetime points earned whilst using each character. Karin is the character used to amass the most lifetime points.

Average Lifetime Score by Character

This chart displays the average lifetime points displayed by character. It takes the the total lifetime points accrued by each character and then divides this by the frequency of which they are used. There is a major difference between the this and the previous charts with certain characters such as Mika and Akuma ranking much higher.
